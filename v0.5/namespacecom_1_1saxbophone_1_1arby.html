Calculates integer log of x in base as bounds of .
Definition: math.hpp:79
Parameters
basebase to use for \(b\)
xvalue to use for \(x\)
Precondition
\(b\geq2\)
\(x\geq1\)
Exceptions
std::domain_errorwhen preconditions are violated
Returns
pair of \((low, high)\) where for \(\mathbb{R}\ v = log_b(x)\):
  • \(low = floor(v)\)
  • \(high = ceil(v)\)
Postcondition
\(low \leq high\)
Remarks
When \(low = high\):
  • \(log_b(x)\in\mathbb{N}\)
Otherwise:
  • \(log_b(x)\in\mathbb{R}\)
Note
Complexity: \( \mathcal{O}(n^2log(n)) \)
